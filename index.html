YOU ARE NOT ALONE




Suicide is the action of a person who takes their own life. Suicide happens for many different reasons. Someone who commits suicide might have depression, have a rough home situation, might be getting bullied, or something else entirely. Suicide happens for many different reasons, but we can try and help.

Why do people turn to suicide?

There are many reasons that a person may think suicide is necesary. Some of the biggest reasons are they are being bullied, have depresion,have realy bad anxiety, or many other things.People may have also had traumatic childhood or life and feel like it is necesary to turn toward suicide. People are more likely to take their own life if they have access to the means to do so.

What are some signs

  • Has already made attempts in the past
  • Talks about wanting to die
  • Felling really hopeless or is in deep despair
  • Talks about self harming a lot
  • Withdrawing from others
  • Difficult life events, such as experiencing a traumatic childhood or physical or emotional abuse
  • Stressful life events like a relationship ending or losing your job
  • Anger at other people, often due to perceived or real mistreatment
  • Excessive use of drugs or alcohol

What can you do?

The most important thing that you can do to help a person is to be there for them. Let them know that they are loved and cared for. It is important that they know that they are not alone and that there is somethhing to live for. It is also important that you contact someone who may be able to help that person in a way that you may not be able to.
